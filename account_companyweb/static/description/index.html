Customers and suppliers will have no secrets for you!

This module provides access to the financial health of Belgian companies that are saved in your database from the client display. All of the information are coming form companyweb BVBA/SPRL database therefore you must have a subscription to companyweb BVBA/SPRL to access to information

Consult informations

Provides information (Name, address, VAT number, credit limit, some financial informations like equity capital, health barometer, some warnings) about Belgian companies which are record your database

Update informations

Update data(address, credit limit) of company which are stored in your database with companyweb data

Share informations

share financial informations with companyweb BVBA/SPRL !

Generate financial reports about your customers which must be provided to companyweb BVBA/SPRL
